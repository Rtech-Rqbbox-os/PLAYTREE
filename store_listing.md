## Description (max 10,000 chars)
PLAYTREE is a stylized multiplayer fantasy adventure game where you awaken in a corrupted world and must restore balance to the ancient forest.

FEATURES:
- 6 unique character classes: Guardian, Ranger, Mage, Mechanic, Beast Tamer, Shadow Assassin
- Massive open world to explore with dynamic weather and day/night cycles
- Boss battles, pet system, mount system, and fishing
- Base building and crafting
- Skill trees and enchanting system
- Daily rewards, achievements, and leaderboards
- Multiplayer support with lobbies
- Battle pass system
- Controller support (Xbox, PlayStation)
- Gamepad and keyboard/mouse input
- Auto-save and manual save system

STORY:
The corruption spreads through the ancient forest. You must cleanse the Root Titan to restore balance. Awaken, rebuild, and restore the world.

Chapter 0 - Season 0: The Awakening begins now.
