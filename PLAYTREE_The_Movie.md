# PLAYTREE: AWAKEN

### An Original Screenplay

**Based on the world of PLAYTREE by RQBBOX Game Studios**

## FADE IN:

### ACT I — THE AWAKENING

**EXT. FLOATING ISLANDS — DAWN**

A slow aerial shot drifts through a fractured sky. Islands of earth, stone, and ancient tree roots hang suspended in an endless void. Some drift lazily. Others are wrapped in tendrils of dark corruption — pulsing, living veins of shadow that cling to rock and bark alike.

**TITLE CARD:** *"The PlayTree once connected all lands. Then the corruption came."*

We descend through clouds of ash and spore toward one island — small, crumbling, barely holding together. At its center stands the stump of a colossal tree, its trunk shattered long ago. Faint green light pulses from within the wood — weak, fading, but alive.

**CLOSE ON** a figure lying face-down in the dirt at the base of the stump.

This is **KAEL** (early 20s, lean, gender-neutral build), dressed in tattered traveler's clothes. Their skin bears faint luminous markings — the sigil of the PlayTree, barely visible, like veins of gold beneath the skin.

Kael's eyes snap open.

**KAEL (V.O.)**
*(breathing, disoriented)*
Where...

They push themselves up. The world around them is silent except for the hum of corruption — a low, insectoid drone that vibrates in the bones.

**KAEL (V.O.)**
I don't remember how I got here. I don't remember anything before this moment.

Kael looks at their hands. The golden sigils pulse faintly.

**KAEL (V.O.)**
But something is calling me. Something ancient. Something dying.

They stand. The ground beneath them is cracked. Through the fissures, green light seeps upward — the last breath of the PlayTree's roots.

**WIDE SHOT** as Kael walks toward the edge of the island. Below: an abyss of clouds and broken land. In the far distance, other floating islands — some green and alive, others black with corruption.

**KAEL (V.O.)**
The world is broken. I can feel it. Every piece of this land is screaming.

A VOICE cuts through the silence.

**ELDER THORNE (O.S.)**
You're awake.

Kael spins. Seated on a root of the stump is **ELDER THORNE** — an old man with deep brown skin, a wide-brimmed hat, and eyes that have seen too much. His clothes are patched but dignified. A staff leans against his knee, carved with the same golden sigils Kael bears.

**KAEL**
Who are you?

**ELDER THORNE**
*(standing slowly)*
I am what's left of the old world. You may call me Thorne.

He gestures to the ruined stump behind them.

**ELDER THORNE**
This was the heart of the PlayTree. Once, its branches touched every island, every shore, every living thing. It was the balance — the thread that held reality together.

**KAEL**
What happened to it?

**ELDER THORNE**
*(darkly)*
The corruption. A force that devours connection. It severed the roots. Shattered the world. Turned living things into... things that hunt.

Thorne walks to the edge of the island. Below, a CORRUPTED BEAST — a twisted, shadow-wreathed creature with glowing red eyes — paces along a lower ledge.

**ELDER THORNE**
Every creature you see out there was once alive. Forest foxes. Crystal dragons. Ancient ents. The corruption twisted them. Made them hostile. Hungry.

**KAEL**
And the Tree?

**ELDER THORNE**
Dying. But not dead. Not yet. There are Five Cores — the anchors of the PlayTree's power. If all five are restored...

He turns to Kael. The golden sigils on Kael's skin flare.

**ELDER THORNE**
...the world heals.

**KAEL**
How do I restore them?

**ELDER THORNE**
*(placing a hand on Kael's shoulder)*
The Tree chose you, Kael. I don't know why. I don't know how. But the moment the corruption came, the Tree poured its last energy into a single soul. Yours.

Beat.

**ELDER THORNE**
You carry the Tree's power in your blood. You are its champion. Its weapon. Its hope.

Kael stares at the stump. The green light within it pulses — stronger now, as if responding to their proximity.

**KAEL**
*(quietly)*
I don't even know what I can do.

**ELDER THORNE**
Then we learn. Together.

Thorne pulls a MAP from his coat — crude, hand-drawn, showing the four regions of the broken world.

**ELDER THORNE**
The first Core lies in the Emerald Roots Forest. The corruption has taken root there — literally. The Root Titan guards the Core. A beast of immense power. Corruption made flesh.

He taps the map.

**ELDER THORNE**
Gather Tree Energy from the forest. It's the lifeblood of the PlayTree — you'll find it glowing in the undergrowth, in the bark of living trees, in the eyes of creatures not yet fully turned.

**KAEL**
And the Titan?

**ELDER THORNE**
*(gravely)*
You'll know it when you see it. And you'll know if you're ready.

### ACT II — THE FOREST AWAKENING

**EXT. EMERALD ROOTS FOREST — DAY**

Kael steps off a crumbling sky-bridge onto solid ground for the first time. The forest stretches endlessly — massive trees with trunks wider than houses, their canopy so thick the sky is barely visible.

But something is wrong.

The trees are dark. Veins of corruption twist through the bark like black roots. The undergrowth is sparse, dying. Where there should be birdsong, there is only that low, droning hum.

**KAEL (V.O.)**
The forest feels... sick. I can feel it in my chest. Like the land itself is in pain.

Kael moves cautiously through the undergrowth. Their hand trails along a tree trunk — and the golden sigils on their skin FLARE. For a brief instant, the tree's bark turns green. Life returns.

Kael pulls their hand away, startled.

**KAEL (V.O.)**
The Tree's energy... it flows through me. Through my touch. I can heal this place.

But before they can experiment further—

A SHRIEK. Something crashes through the undergrowth.

**CORRUPTED SAPLING** — a twisted, humanoid plant-creature with thorned limbs and glowing red eyes — lunges at Kael.

Kael scrambles backward. The creature swings. Kael rolls, barely avoiding the strike.

**KAEL**
*(panicking)*
Thorne said gather energy — he didn't say anything about—

The sapling lunges again. Kael grabs a fallen branch and swings instinctively. It connects. The creature stumbles.

And where Kael's branch touched it, the corruption RECOILS. Green light erupts from the wound. The sapling SCREAMS — and then dissolves into motes of green energy that flow into Kael's hand.

**KAEL (V.O.)**
*(breathing hard)*
Tree Energy. That's what that is. The corruption... and the Tree... they're opposites. When I fight the corruption, the Tree absorbs it.

Kael looks at their hand. The golden sigils are brighter.

**KAEL (V.O.)**
I'm getting stronger. The more I cleanse, the more power I have.

**MONTAGE — Kael's journey through the Forest:**

- Kael fights CORRUPTED BEASTS and SHADOW ROOTS, learning to dodge, strike, and use the Tree's energy as a weapon
- They find VINE CRAWLERS hiding in ambush — and learn to anticipate, to read the forest
- They discover MOSS GOLEMS — massive, slow, ancient protectors now corrupted. Kael must find their weak points
- Along the way, they gather TREE ENERGY — glowing green orbs hidden in hollow logs, floating in pools, embedded in bark
- Kael builds a small CAMP at the base of a living tree, the first one they've fully healed. Firelight. Safety. The corruption recoils from the light

**EXT. FOREST CLEARING — NIGHT**

Kael sits by their campfire. The living tree behind them hums with energy. Around the clearing, corrupted creatures pace in the darkness — but they won't cross the boundary of green light.

**KAEL**
*(to themselves)*
Every tree I heal pushes the corruption back. Every creature I cleanse gives me more energy. But there are so many...

A sound. Footsteps.

Kael spins, weapon raised.

From the shadows emerges **VEYRA** — a woman in green and brown leathers, a bow slung across her back, her face painted with forest sigils. She's a SCOUT — sharp-eyed, quick-moving, and wary.

**VEYRA**
Easy. I'm not one of them.

**KAEL**
*(not lowering weapon)*
How do I know that?

**VEYRA**
*(smirking)*
Because I'm not glowing red and trying to eat you.

Beat. Kael lowers their weapon.

**VEYRA**
I've been watching you. You're... different. The corruption doesn't stick to you. You touch the trees and they wake up.

**KAEL**
I'm... I don't fully understand it myself.

**VEYRA**
*(sitting across the fire)*
Name's Veyra. I mapped the Skyroot Peaks before the corruption took the passes. I've been surviving down here since.

She pulls out a rough MAP — similar to Thorne's, but with more detail.

**VEYRA**
The Root Titan is real. I've seen it. Massive thing — the size of a cathedral. It moves through the earth like water. Corrupted roots wrap around everything it touches.

She points to a location deep in the forest.

**VEYRA**
It guards the first Tree Core. In a cavern beneath the Great Stump. That's the heart of this forest — or what's left of it.

**KAEL**
Have you fought it?

**VEYRA**
*(laughing bitterly)*
Once. From a distance. It didn't even notice me. I wasn't worth its attention.

Beat.

**VEYRA**
You might be.

### ACT III — THE ROOT TITAN

**EXT. GREAT STUMP — DAY**

Kael and Veyra approach the heart of the forest. The Great Stump is enormous — a dead tree trunk the size of a building, its roots spreading across the ground like rivers. But the roots are black. Corrupted. Pulsing.

At the base of the stump, a CAVERN MOUTH yawns open. Green light flickers from within — the Tree Core.

And between them and the cavern: THE ROOT TITAN.

It rises from the earth like a mountain becoming alive. A colossus of twisted wood, stone, and corruption. Its body is a shifting mass of roots and tendrils. Its head — if it can be called that — is a crown of shattered branches, with a single glowing RED EYE at its center.

**ROOT TITAN (V.O.)**
*(a deep, grinding voice that echoes through the earth)*
**YOU CARRY THE TREE'S FLAME. I WILL SMOTHER IT.**

**KAEL**
*(to Veyra)*
Stay behind me.

**VEYRA**
*(drawing her bow)*
Not a chance.

**THE BATTLE BEGINS.**

The Root Titan SLAMS its fist into the ground. The earth erupts — ROOT TENDRILS burst upward, chasing Kael across the battlefield. Kael dodges, rolls, fights.

Veyra fires arrow after arrow, each one carrying a hint of green energy. The tendrils RECOIL from her shots.

**KAEL (V.O.)**
It's fast for something that size. And the corruption — it's using the corruption to heal itself. Every time I wound it, the dark roots regrow.

The Titan transitions to PHASE 2. Its eye brightens. It ROARS — a sound that shakes the trees. Now it uses ROOT SLAM with more force, sending shockwaves that crumble the earth.

**KAEL**
*(dodging a slam)*
It's getting stronger!

**VEYRA**
*(firing)*
It's using the Core's power against us! The corruption feeds on it!

Kael looks at the cavern behind the Titan. The green light within is pulsing — fast, desperate.

**KAEL (V.O.)**
The Core is calling to me. It wants to be freed. But the Titan is in the way.

Kael charges. The Titan swings. Kael dodges — barely — and drives their weapon into the Titan's leg. Green light ERUPTS from the wound. The Titan SCREAMS.

But the corruption fights back. Dark roots wrap around Kael's arm, trying to pull them in. The golden sigils on Kael's skin FLARE — burning the corruption away, but the pain is excruciating.

**KAEL**
*(screaming)*
I can feel it — the corruption — it's trying to consume me—

**VEYRA**
*(screaming)*
FIGHT IT!

Kael pushes deeper. The golden light intensifies. The corruption RECOILS.

The Titan enters PHASE 3 — ENRAGE. It grows LARGER. Its eye turns blood red. The ground beneath it cracks and splits.

**ROOT TITAN**
**YOU CANNOT DEFEAT WHAT THE CORRUPTION HAS CLAIMED. I AM ETERNAL.**

**KAEL**
*(standing firm, glowing with golden light)*
No. You're not.

Kael raises both hands. The Tree's energy pours through them — a BEAM of green light that strikes the Titan dead center. The corruption SHATTERS. The Titan SCREAMS — a sound that echoes across the forest.

And then — silence.

The Titan COLLAPSES. Its body dissolves into motes of green and black energy. The corruption evaporates. The ground heals. Trees begin to GROW where the Titan stood.

Kael falls to their knees, exhausted. Veyra rushes to their side.

**VEYRA**
You did it. You actually did it.

Kael looks up. The cavern mouth is open now, unguarded. The green light within is BLAZING.

**KAEL**
*(weakly)*
The Core...

They crawl into the cavern. At its center, floating above a pedestal of living wood: THE FOREST CORE — a crystal of pure green energy, pulsing with the rhythm of a heartbeat.

Kael reaches out. Their hand touches the Core.

A VISION —

**FLASH — The PlayTree in its prime.** Towering above the world. Its branches stretching to every horizon. Creatures of all kinds living beneath it in harmony. The world whole. Connected. Alive.

**FLASH — The Corruption arrives.** A dark wave, sweeping across the land. The Tree's roots shatter. The islands break apart. Creatures twist and scream.

**FLASH — The PlayTree's final act.** It pours its energy into a single point — a human figure, lying at its base. Kael.

**ELDER THORNE (V.O.)**
*(from the vision)*
The Tree chose you because you are its bridge. Between what was and what could be. Between corruption and balance.

The vision fades. Kael opens their eyes. The Forest Core is gone — absorbed into them. Their entire body GLOWS with green light. The sigils are no longer faint. They are BRIGHT. BRILLIANT.

**KAEL (V.O.)**
*(quietly, awestruck)*
One down. Four to go.

**EXT. GREAT STUMP — CONTINUOUS**

Kael emerges from the cavern. The forest around them is HEALING. Leaves return to branches. Flowers bloom. The corruption retreats.

Veyra stares.

**VEYRA**
The forest... it's waking up.

**KAEL**
*(looking at their glowing hands)*
It's just the beginning.

Behind them, in the distance, the sky shifts. The corruption recoils — but then surges BACK, angrier, denser. As if the loss of the first Core has awakened something worse.

**VEYRA**
*(pointing)*
Kael... look.

On the horizon, through a gap in the trees: a MASSIVE CAVE SYSTEM, its entrance glowing with purple crystal light. Deep within, something MOVES. Something with many heads.

**TITLE CARD:** *"Round 2: Crystal Depths"*

### ACT IV — CRYSTAL DEPTHS

**EXT. CRYSTAL HOLLOW — DAY**

Kael and Veyra stand at the entrance to the Crystal Hollow. The walls are lined with amethyst and quartz — beautiful, but corrupted. Dark veins pulse through the crystals.

**VEYRA**
The Crystal Hollow. Home of the Crystal Hydra. It guards the second Core.

**KAEL**
What do we know about it?

**VEYRA**
It's fast. It breathes crystal energy that shatters everything it touches. And when you wound it...

**KAEL**
What?

**VEYRA**
It splits. Each head becomes two. The more you fight it, the more heads it has.

**KAEL**
*(grimly)*
Great.

They enter the caves. The crystals around them glow — some green, most corrupted purple. Kael reaches out and touches a crystal. It SHATTERS — and inside, a CLUE. A carved rune. An ancient message.

**KAEL (V.O.)**
The old world left traces. Messages in the crystal. They knew the corruption was coming. They tried to warn people. But no one listened.

They push deeper. The caves are a labyrinth — passages twist and branch. Corrupted CRYSTAL MONSTERS emerge from the walls. CRYSTAL GOLEMS block their path. PRISM MOTHS swarm in the darkness.

**MONTAGE — Kael and Veyra fight through the Crystal Hollow:**

- Kael uses the Tree's energy to shatter corrupted crystals, clearing paths
- Veyra's arrows find weak points in Crystal Golem armor
- They discover CRYSTAL BAT nests — and learn to fight in tight spaces
- They find a PRISM LURKER — a teleporting crystal predator — and nearly die before figuring out its pattern
- Along the way, Kael finds CRYSTAL DUST — the second resource, glowing purple — and begins to understand how to use it for ENCHANTING

**INT. CRYSTAL CAVERN — THE HYDRA'S LAIR**

The cavern is vast. Crystals jut from every surface like teeth. In the center, coiled around a PEDESTAL of pure crystal, is the CRYSTAL HYDRA.

It's enormous — a serpentine body with THREE HEADS, each one wreathed in crystalline armor. Its eyes glow purple. When it breathes, shards of crystal energy shoot from its mouths.

**CRYSTAL HYDRA**
*(a chittering, multi-tonal voice)*
**FOOLS. YOU BRING THE TREE'S FLAME INTO MY DOMAIN? I WILL SHATTER IT. I WILL SHATTER YOU.**

The Hydra STRIKES. Crystal breath — a beam of purple energy — shatters the ground where Kael stood a second ago.

Veyra fires. Her arrow strikes one of the heads — and SPLITS IT. The single head becomes TWO. The Hydra now has FOUR.

**VEYRA**
*(horrified)*
I told you.

**KAEL**
New plan — don't wound it. Disable it.

Kael charges, but not at the heads — at the BODY. They slam their weapon into the Hydra's midsection. Green light erupts. The Hydra SCREAMS.

But the heads recover. They converge on Kael — crystal breath from three directions. Kael rolls, dodges, barely survives.

**KAEL (V.O.)**
It's faster than the Titan. And smarter. The corruption gave it cunning.

The Hydra enters PHASE 2 — SPLIT HEADS. All four heads now attack independently, coordinating, trapping Kael and Veyra in a crossfire of crystal energy.

**VEYRA**
*(dodging)*
It's herding us!

**KAEL**
*(realizing)*
The Core. It's protecting the Core. If we can get past it—

Kael makes a choice. They grab Veyra and RUN — straight at the Hydra.

**VEYRA**
ARE YOU INSANE?!

**KAEL**
TRUST ME!

The Hydra rears back to strike — and Kael activates the Tree's energy at FULL POWER. A SHOCKWAVE of green light erupts from their body. The crystal heads SHATTER — not split, SHATTER. The corruption in the crystals BURNS away.

The Hydra enters PHASE 3 — ENRAGE. Its remaining heads GLOW red. It grows LARGER. It summons CRYSTAL SHARD RAIN — thousands of crystal fragments rain from the ceiling.

Kael and Veyra dive for cover. The shards embed in the ground around them.

**KAEL**
*(bleeding, exhausted)*
One more hit. I need one more hit.

**VEYRA**
*(covering them with arrow fire)*
Then TAKE it!

Kael sprints through the crystal rain. A shard cuts their arm. Another their leg. They don't stop.

They reach the Hydra's body and DRIVE their weapon deep into its core.

**GREEN LIGHT ERUPTS.**

The Hydra WRITHES. The corruption DETONATES outward — and then collapses inward, sucked into the wound. The Hydra's body turns to crystal — then to dust — then to nothing.

Silence.

Kael collapses. Veyra catches them.

**VEYRA**
I've got you. I've got you.

They look up. The Crystal Core floats above the pedestal — a crystal of pure purple-green energy, pulsing with warmth.

Kael reaches out. Touches it.

**ANOTHER VISION —**

**FLASH — The Crystal Hydra, before corruption.** A beautiful, gentle creature. Three heads, each one curious, playful. It explored the caves, collecting crystals, building its lair with care.

**FLASH — The Corruption arrives.** It seeps into the crystals. The Hydra fights it — but it's too strong. The corruption twists the creature, turns its beauty into a weapon.

**FLASH — The Hydra's final moment of clarity.** Before the corruption fully takes it, the Hydra looks at the Core and WHISPERS: *"Free us."*

Kael opens their eyes. The Crystal Core is absorbed. Their sigils now glow both GREEN and PURPLE.

**KAEL (V.O.)**
Two down. Three to go.

**EXT. CRYSTAL HOLLOW — EXIT**

They emerge from the caves. The crystal formations around them are HEALING — the purple corruption fading, replaced by natural crystal light.

But the sky above is DARKER. Storm clouds gather. Lightning crackles.

**VEYRA**
*(looking up)*
The corruption is pushing back. Harder than before.

**KAEL**
*(standing, still glowing with two colors)*
Then we push harder.

On the horizon: a MOUNTAIN RANGE, its peaks lost in perpetual storm. Lightning strikes the summits. Wind howls.

**TITLE CARD:** *"Round 3: Storm Peaks"*

### ACT V — THE STORM PEAKS

**EXT. SKYROOT PEAKS — NIGHT**

The mountain pass is brutal. Wind tears at Kael and Veyra. Rain lashes sideways. Thunder booms so close the ground shakes.

**VEYRA**
*(shouting over the wind)*
The Skyroot Peaks! Home of the Sky Guardian! It guards the last source of pure light!

**KAEL**
*(shouting back)*
How do we get up there?!

**VEYRA**
We climb!

They ascend. The storm WORSENS — not natural storm. Corruption storm. Dark lightning. Rain that burns. Wind that whispers with the voices of corrupted creatures.

**MONTAGE — The ascent:**

- Kael and Veyra climb sheer cliff faces, dodging STORM HAWKS — fast, corrupted birds that dive-bomb with razor talons
- They fight CLOUD SERPENTS — massive, corrupted sky creatures that wrap around ledges and try to pull them off
- They discover TEMPEST MAGES — corrupted spellcasters who hurl lightning from the clouds
- They find THUNDER BEASTS — massive, armored creatures that charge through the storm
- At a campsite on a narrow ledge, Kael begins to MASTER their abilities — combining Tree energy with Crystal energy for new attacks
- Veyra teaches Kael to read the storm — to find patterns, to use the corruption's own chaos against it

**EXT. SUMMIT — THE SKY GUARDIAN'S ROOST**

They reach the peak. Above the clouds, the storm clears — for a moment. Stars are visible. And at the center of the summit, floating in a column of pure light: THE SKY CORE.

Guarding it: THE SKY GUARDIAN.

It's not a beast. It's a WARRIOR — ancient, spectral, clad in armor of light and cloud. Its wings spread across the sky. Its eyes burn with golden fire. It was once the protector of the heavens. Now it's corrupted — but only partially. Its armor is cracked. Light leaks from the wounds.

**SKY GUARDIAN**
*(a voice like thunder)*
**WHO DARES APPROACH THE LAST LIGHT?**

**KAEL**
I'm here to free you. To free the Core. To restore balance.

**SKY GUARDIAN**
*(raising its blade)*
**BALANCE IS A MEMORY. LIGHT IS A LIE. THE CORRUPTION SHOWED ME TRUTH: ALL THINGS END. ALL THINGS DARKEN.**

**KAEL**
That's not truth. That's despair.

**SKY GUARDIAN**
**THEN PROVE ME WRONG.**

**THE BATTLE OF THE STORM PEAKS.**

The Sky Guardian fights with WIND GUSTS — sending Kael and Veyra tumbling. LIGHTNING STRIKES — calling bolts from the sky. SKY DIVE — launching into the air and crashing down with devastating force.

This is the hardest fight yet. The Guardian is FAST. PRECISE. It fights like a GENERAL — reading every move, countering every strategy.

**VEYRA**
*(dodging a gust)*
It's not just strong — it's SMART. It's been fighting for centuries!

**KAEL**
*(thinking)*
It's also in pain. The corruption is cracking its armor. If I can reach the cracks...

Kael makes a desperate play. They CLIMB the Sky Guardian — literally, scaling its armor as it flies. The Guardian tries to shake them off. Lightning crackles around Kael's body.

**SKY GUARDIAN**
**FOOL! YOU CANNOT CLIMB THE STORM!**

**KAEL**
*(digging their fingers into a crack in the armor)*
I'm not climbing the storm. I'm climbing YOU.

Kael reaches the CRACK — the wound where corruption meets light. They place both hands on it and channel EVERYTHING. Tree energy. Crystal energy. All of it.

**GREEN AND PURPLE LIGHT ERUPTS from the wound.**

The Sky Guardian SCREAMS — not in anger, but in RELIEF. The corruption BURNS away. The armor HEALS. The cracks seal.

The Guardian lands. Kael falls from its back — but catches themselves.

The Sky Guardian stands tall. Its armor is whole. Its eyes are clear. For the first time in centuries, it sees the world without corruption.

**SKY GUARDIAN**
*(softly, gratefully)*
**...Thank you.**

It reaches into its own chest and draws out the SKY CORE — a crystal of pure golden light.

**SKY GUARDIAN**
**Take it. Restore what was broken. I will guard this peak no longer... but I will watch. And when the world is whole again, I will sing.**

Kael takes the Core. It merges with them. Their sigils now glow GREEN, PURPLE, and GOLD.

**KAEL (V.O.)**
Three down. Two to go.

But as the Sky Guardian fades — dissolving into light — the sky DARKENS again. Not from storm. From something WORSE.

**A VOICE — deep, ancient, malevolent — echoes from everywhere and nowhere:**

**THE HOLLOW KING (V.O.)**
**You think you're restoring balance? You're feeding me. Every Core you absorb makes you stronger... and makes ME stronger. You carry the Tree's power inside you. And when I consume you... I will consume IT.**

Kael staggers. The sigils on their body FLARE — painfully.

**KAEL**
*(clutching their chest)*
What... what is this?

**VEYRA**
*(alarmed)*
Kael! What's happening?!

**KAEL**
*(gasping)*
The Hollow King... it's been watching. It's been WAITING.

Beat.

**KAEL (V.O.)**
He's right. Every Core I absorb... the corruption inside me grows too. The Tree's power and the corruption are two sides of the same coin. If I take all five Cores... I'll either save the world or become the final corruption.

**VEYRA**
Then we don't let that happen. We fight. Together.

**KAEL**
*(looking at Veyra)*
Veyra... if I can't control it—

**VEYRA**
*(fiercely)*
You WILL control it. Because I'll be there to remind you who you are.

Beat.

**KAEL**
*(small smile)*
Together.

**EXT. HORIZON — THE HOLLOW THRONE**

In the far distance, a FORTRESS of corruption rises from the broken land. A throne of shadow and void. The source of all corruption. The seat of THE HOLLOW KING.

**TITLE CARD:** *"Round 4: The Hollow Throne"*

### ACT VI — THE HOLLOW THRONE

**EXT. THE CORRUPTED WASTES — DAY (BUT DARK)**

The land between the Peaks and the Throne is DEAD. No trees. No life. Just black earth, corrupted stone, and the ever-present drone of the Hollow King's presence.

Kael and Veyra walk side by side. Both are wounded, exhausted, but determined.

**VEYRA**
*(quietly)*
Kael... I need to tell you something.

**KAEL**
What?

**VEYRA**
I've been fighting the corruption since it came. I've lost people. Friends. Family. I thought I'd never see the world whole again.

She stops. Turns to Kael.

**VEYRA**
You gave me hope. Whatever happens in there... thank you.

**KAEL**
*(taking her hand)*
We're walking out of there. Both of us.

They continue.

**EXT. THE HOLLOW THRONE — ENTRANCE**

The fortress is a cathedral of corruption. Massive walls of twisted black wood and living shadow. The entrance is a MAW — a gaping maw of darkness, lined with teeth of corrupted crystal.

Kael and Veyra enter.

**INT. THE HOLLOW THRONE — CORRIDOR**

The corridor is alive. The walls PULSE. Shadow creatures — SHADOW WRAITHS — emerge from the darkness, attacking in waves.

**MONTAGE — The gauntlet to the Throne:**

- Kael fights SHADOW WRAITHS — teleporting, soul-draining horrors
- Veyra holds the rear, her arrows now infused with Tree and Crystal energy
- They encounter DARK KNIGHTS — massive, corrupted warriors in black armor
- SHADOW MAGES hurl dark energy from the walls
- VOID STALKERS emerge from shadows — Kael must use every skill they've learned
- INFERNO HOUNDS — corrupted fire creatures — guard the inner sanctum
- Kael absorbs shadow energy as they fight — the corruption growing inside them, but they CONTROL it

**INT. THE HOLLOW THRONE — THE THRONE ROOM**

The room is vast. A throne of pure corruption sits at the center — a living thing, pulsing, breathing. And upon it: THE HOLLOW KING.

He is MASSIVE. Not a creature — a CONCEPT given form. His body is shadow and void, with a crown of corrupted branches — a mockery of the PlayTree. His eyes are empty. His mouth is a wound in reality.

**THE HOLLOW KING**
*(the voice of the void itself)*
**AT LAST. THE TREE'S CHAMPION STANDS BEFORE ME.**

**KAEL**
*(standing firm)*
This ends now.

**THE HOLLOW KING**
**DOES IT? YOU CARRY THREE CORES INSIDE YOU. THREE PIECES OF BALANCE. BUT BALANCE...**

He raises a hand. The shadows around Kael INTENSIFY. The corruption in Kael's body SCREAMS.

**THE HOLLOW KING**
**...REQUIRES SOMETHING TO BALANCE AGAINST.**

**KAEL**
*(clutching their chest, the sigils flickering)*
You're inside me. The corruption... it's been growing...

**THE HOLLOW KING**
**EVERY CORE YOU ABSORBED CAME WITH A COST. THE TREE'S POWER AND MY POWER ARE TWO HALVES OF THE SAME WHOLE. YOU ARE THE BRIDGE, KAEL. AND BRIDGES... CAN BE BROKEN.**

**VEYRA**
*(stepping forward, bow drawn)*
Kael! Don't listen to him!

**THE HOLLOW KING**
*(ignoring her)*
**FIFTH CORE. THE HOLLOW CORE. IT IS INSIDE ME. TAKE IT... AND YOU BECOME THE BALANCE. OR YOU BECOME THE CORRUPTION. THERE IS NO IN-BETWEEN.**

Beat.

**KAEL**
*(to Veyra)*
This is it. The final choice.

**VEYRA**
*(tearfully)*
You don't have to—

**KAEL**
I do. The Tree chose me because I'm the bridge. Between light and dark. Between life and corruption. I have to accept BOTH.

Kael walks toward the Hollow King. The shadows try to stop them. Veyra fires arrow after arrow, holding them back.

**THE HOLLOW KING**
**COME THEN, CHAMPION. TAKE WHAT IS YOURS.**

Kael reaches the Hollow King. They place their hands on the King's chest — and PULL.

The HOLLOW CORE erupts from the King — a crystal of pure black and white energy, swirling together.

**THE FINAL VISION —**

Kael sees EVERYTHING. The creation of the world. The PlayTree growing. The corruption arriving — not as an enemy, but as a SYMPTOM. The world became unbalanced — too much light, too much order, too much control. The corruption was the world's ATTEMPT to restore balance — but it went too far. Became destructive. Became the very thing it was trying to fix.

**THE TREE (V.O.)**
*(a voice of infinite calm)*
Balance is not the absence of darkness. It is the acceptance of both. Light and shadow. Life and decay. Creation and destruction. You are the bridge, Kael. Not between good and evil — between WHAT IS and WHAT COULD BE.

Kael opens their eyes. They hold the Hollow Core — black and white swirling together. They absorb it.

**ALL FIVE CORES ACTIVATE.**

Kael's body TRANSFORMS. Their sigils glow with ALL FIVE COLORS — green, purple, gold, blue, and the black-white of the Hollow Core. They rise into the air. Light and shadow dance around them.

**THE HOLLOW KING**
*(for the first time, uncertain)*
**WHAT... WHAT ARE YOU?**

**KAEL**
*(their voice layered with all five Cores)*
I am the Balance.

Kael raises a hand. The corruption in the room BURNS — not destroyed, but TRANSFORMED. The Hollow King's body WRITHES.

**THE HOLLOW KING**
**NO! I AM ETERNAL! I AM THE VOID!**

**KAEL**
You're fear. You're despair. You're the belief that nothing can be saved.

Kael drives their hand into the Hollow King's chest.

**KAEL**
But I've seen what the world was. And I know what it can be again.

**GREEN LIGHT ERUPTS.**

The Hollow King SCREAMS — a sound that shakes the world. His body SHATTERS. The corruption DETONATES — outward, then inward, collapsing into a single point of light that flows into Kael.

**SILENCE.**

**EXT. THE BROKEN WORLD — DAWN**

Kael falls from the sky. Veyra catches them.

They lie on the ground, breathing. The sigils on their body GLOW — softly, steadily. All five colors, in harmony.

**VEYRA**
*(crying)*
You did it. Kael... you did it.

**KAEL**
*(weakly)*
Not yet.

Kael stands. They walk to the center of the broken land. Around them, the world is still dead. Still corrupted. Still broken.

Kael raises both hands.

**KAEL**
*(to the world, to the Tree, to everything)*
WAKE UP.

**THE RESTORATION.**

From Kael's hands, ROOTS of pure light shoot outward — not the twisted roots of corruption, but the living roots of the PlayTree. They spread across the land. Through the earth. Under the water. Into the sky.

The corruption BURNS — not with destruction, but with TRANSFORMATION. Dark becomes light. Shadow becomes shade. Corruption becomes soil.

**ELDER THORNE (V.O.)**

Balance is restored. The world remembers what it was. And now... it knows what it can be.

What now?
